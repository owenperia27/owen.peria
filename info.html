Biographical elements

Based in Paris, Owen Peria is a contemporary photographer and filmmaker capturing the intersection of raw kinetic energy and highly curated visual storytelling.

From the vibrant stage chemistry of world-class musicians to the sophisticated, sharp aesthetic required by premium hospitality and spirit brands, his lens focuses on the precise tension between light, atmosphere, and movement.
